## Why KiCad?

KiCad is free, open-source, and genuinely capable of professional-grade PCB design. After trying Eagle and EasyEDA, I settled on KiCad for all my hobby projects.

## The Workflow

1. **Schematic capture** - Draw your circuit, assign footprints
2. **PCB layout** - Place components, route traces
3. **Design rule check** - Catch errors before fabrication
4. **Generate Gerbers** - Export manufacturing files
5. **Order boards** - Upload to JLCPCB, OSH Park, or PCBWay

## Tips for Beginners

- Start with a simple project (breakout board or LED driver)
- Use the built-in footprint libraries before making custom ones
- Run DRC frequently, not just at the end
- Add mounting holes and fiducials from the start
- Keep your ground planes solid
